# China-Specific Industry Graph (2024-2026)

## 1. Inside the System (泛体制内)
- **Central/State-owned Enterprises (国央企):** High stability, compliance-heavy, structured progression. 
  - *Key Strengths:* Consistency, Responsibility, Harmony, Deliberative.
- **Public Service/Civil Servants (考公考编/文职):** Absolute stability, process-oriented, service mindset.
  - *Key Strengths:* Discipline, Belief, Focus, Relator.

## 2. Emerging Technology (兴起科技)
- **Big Models & AI (大模型/人工智能):** High innovation, rapid iteration, uncertainty.
  - *Key Strengths:* Ideation, Learner, Strategic, Activator.
- **Semiconductors (半导体/芯片):** High technical threshold, long cycle, precision-required.
  - *Key Strengths:* Analytical, Restorative, Focus, Deliberative.
- **New Energy (新能源/电动车):** Industrial scaling, policy-driven, manufacturing excellence.
  - *Key Strengths:* Maximizer, Command, Arranger, Competitive.

## 3. Advanced Manufacturing & Bio (先进制造/生物医药)
- **Advanced Manufacturing (先进制造):** Smart factories, robotics, efficiency.
  - *Key Strengths:* Arranger, Focus, Discipline, Consistency.
- **Biotech (生物医药):** R&D intensive, high risk, long-term impact.
  - *Key Strengths:* Analytical, Futuristic, Intellection, Input.

## 4. Modern Services & Digital (现代服务/数字化)
- **Internet/Software (互联网/大厂):** Peak efficiency, high pressure, "996" or similar high-intensity.
  - *Key Strengths:* Competition, Achiever, Maximizer, Communication.
- **Fintech (金融科技):** Data-driven, market-sensitive, tech+finance hybrid.
  - *Key Strengths:* Analytical, Strategic, Self-Assurance, Significance.
- **FMCG (快消):** Brand-driven, fast pace, market-centric. (Note: Excluding "New Consumption" sub-segments as per user request).
  - *Key Strengths:* Woo, Positivity, Empathy, Individualization.

## 5. Entrepreneurship & Content (创业/自媒体)
- **Self-Media/Content Creation (自媒体):** High autonomy, creative-heavy, immediate feedback.
  - *Key Strengths:* Woo, Ideation, Activator, Positivity.
- **Entrepreneurship (创业):** High risk, vision-driven, resource integration.
  - *Key Strengths:* Command, Self-Assurance, Strategic, Achiever.

## 6. Education & Academic (教育/学术)
- **Education (教育培训):** Skill transfer, human-centric.
  - *Key Strengths:* Developer, Empathy, Communication, Learner.
