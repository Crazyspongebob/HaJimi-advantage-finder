# Gallup CliftonStrengths: 34 Themes & 4 Domains

## [1] Executing (执行力)
People with dominant Executing themes know how to make things happen.
- **Achiever (成就):** Intense drive to complete tasks.
- **Arranger (统筹):** Organizing resources for maximum efficiency.
- **Belief (信仰):** Core values that provide purpose.
- **Consistency (公平):** Treating everyone exactly the same.
- **Deliberative (审慎):** Serious care in making decisions/choices.
- **Discipline (纪律):** Love for structure and routine.
- **Focus (专注):** Setting and following through on goals.
- **Responsibility (责任):** Psychological ownership for commitments.
- **Restorative (排难):** Adept at dealing with problems.

## [2] Influencing (影响力)
People with dominant Influencing themes know how to take charge, speak up and make sure the team is heard.
- **Activator (行动):** Making things happen by turning thoughts into action.
- **Command (统帅):** Presence to take control of a situation.
- **Communication (沟通):** Putting thoughts into words easily.
- **Competition (竞争):** Measuring progress against others.
- **Maximizer (完美):** Focusing on strengths to stimulate personal/team excellence.
- **Self-Assurance (自信):** Faith in their ability to manage their own lives.
- **Significance (追求):** Wanting to be very important in others' eyes.
- **Woo (取悦):** Winning others over; love the challenge of meeting new people.

## [3] Relationship Building (关系建立)
People with dominant Relationship Building themes have the ability to build strong relationships that can hold a team together.
- **Adaptability (适应):** Living in the "now"; taking things as they come.
- **Connectedness (关联):** Faith in the links between all things.
- **Developer (伯乐):** Recognizing and cultivating the potential in others.
- **Empathy (体谅):** Sensing the feelings of other people.
- **Harmony (和谐):** Looking for consensus; avoiding conflict.
- **Includer (包容):** Acceptance of others; show awareness of those who feel left out.
- **Individualization (个别):** Intrigued by the unique qualities of each person.
- **Positivity (积极):** Enthusiastic and upbeat.
- **Relator (交往):** Enjoying close relationships with others.

## [4] Strategic Thinking (战略思维)
People with dominant Strategic Thinking themes help teams consider what could be by absorbing and analyzing information.
- **Analytical (分析):** Searching for reasons and causes.
- **Context (回顾):** Thinking about the past to understand the present.
- **Futuristic (前瞻):** Inspired by the future and what could be.
- **Ideation (理念):** Fascinated by ideas; finding connections between disparate phenomena.
- **Input (搜集):** Craving to know more; like to collect and archive all kinds of information.
- **Intellection (思维):** Characterized by intellectual activity; introspective.
- **Learner (学习):** Great desire to learn and want to continuously improve.
- **Strategic (战略):** Creating alternative ways to proceed.
