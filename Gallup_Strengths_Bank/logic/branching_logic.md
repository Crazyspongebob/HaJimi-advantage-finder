# UG vs PG Branching Logic

## Phase 1: Identity Splitting
The system detects the user's current status (Undergraduate or Graduate) and branches the situational logic accordingly.

### [Branch A] Undergraduate (UG) - 本科生典型场景
Focus on campus life, peer interaction, and foundational choices.
- **Scenario 1: Student Organizations (社团/学生会):** Decision making in small teams, event planning.
- **Scenario 2: Group Projects (小组作业):** Responsibility sharing, conflict resolution, leader vs. follower roles.
- **Scenario 3: Internships (基础实习):** Initial exposure to professional rules, basic skill application.
- **Scenario 4: Post-Grad Exam (考研/保研):** Long-term goal persistence, resource searching.
- **Tone:** Youthful, exploratory, slightly casual.

### [Branch B] Graduate (PG) - 研究生进阶场景
Focus on academic depth, professional management, and high-stakes decisions.
- **Scenario 1: Lab/Project Management (实验室项目管理):** Managing resources, younger students, and high-value equipment.
- **Scenario 2: Academic Research (学术研究):** Depth of thought, handle failed experiments, data analysis.
- **Scenario 3: Advisor Communication (导师沟通):** High-stakes interpersonal negotiation, expectation management.
- **Scenario 4: Paper Publication (核心期刊发表):** Persistence through revision cycles, perfectionism.
- **Tone:** Professional, analytical, slightly reflective, "mentor-like" or "colleague-like".

## Analysis Signal Capture
- If the user mentions "DDL" (Deadline), check: *Achiever, Discipline, Focus.*
- If the user mentions "My tutor" (导师) or "Lab" (实验室), check: *Responsibility, Relator, Input.*
- If the user mentions "Socializing" or "Winning over peers", check: *Woo, Communication, Command.*
