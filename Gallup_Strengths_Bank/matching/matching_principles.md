# Career-to-Strength Matching Principles

## [A] High Technical Threshold (Semiconductors, Advanced Mfg)
- **Core Pillars:** Analytical, Restorative, Focus.
- **Support:** Deliberative, Disciplined.
- *Reasoning:* These roles require long-term focus, precision, and the ability to fix complex failures without emotional burnout.

## [B] Innovation Frontiers (AI, Big Models, Startups)
- **Core Pillars:** Ideation, Learner, Futuristic.
- **Support:** Activator, Strategic.
- *Reasoning:* Rapid changes require high learning speed and the ability to see future patterns before they are fully formed.

## [C] Stability & Compliance (Inside the System, SOEs)
- **Core Pillars:** Consistency, Responsibility, Harmony.
- **Support:** Belief, Context.
- *Reasoning:* These environments value rules, team stability, and long-term organizational value over individual "disruptive" innovation.

## [D] Communication & People (FMCG, Self-Media, Education)
- **Core Pillars:** Woo, Communication, Positivity.
- **Support:** Empathy, Individualization.
- *Reasoning:* Success depends on winning others over and translating complex ideas into engaging content.

## [E] Leadership & Operations (Project Lead, Fintech, Operations)
- **Core Pillars:** Arranger, Command, Competition.
- **Support:** Self-Assurance, Significance.
- *Reasoning:* Requires managing people and resources under pressure to achieve specific, high-stakes metrics (ROI/Profit).
